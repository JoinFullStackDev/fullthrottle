## 3. Account Management Activities

### Ongoing Touchpoints
- [ ] Regular check-in cadence (define by tier)
- [ ] Product usage reviews (are they using all features?)
- [ ] Provider network growth tracking (are more providers being added?)
- [ ] Prescription volume trend monitoring
- [ ] Renewals management (60–90 days before renewal)

### Expansion Opportunities
- [ ] Additional pharmacy locations
- [ ] Increased provider seats / tier upgrades
- [ ] New modules / add-ons as released
- [ ] Referrals to other pharmacies

### Risk Management
- [ ] Health score framework (define what "healthy" vs. "at-risk" looks like)
- [ ] Churn signals: login frequency drops, support ticket volume spikes, no provider invitations sent
- [ ] Escalation playbook for at-risk accounts
