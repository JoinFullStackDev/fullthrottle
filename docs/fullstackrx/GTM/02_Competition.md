## 1. Competitive Landscape Overview

> 🔴 **TBD — Requires market research**

The compounding pharmacy software market is fragmented. Most pharmacies rely on:
- Legacy pharmacy management systems (not built for compounding)
- Generic practice management tools
- Manual workflows (phone/fax/email for prescriptions)
- Combinations of point solutions (separate for prescriptions, patients, billing)

**FullStackRx's differentiation hypothesis:** Purpose-built for compounding, multi-stakeholder (pharmacy + provider + patient), modern UX, HIPAA-native.
