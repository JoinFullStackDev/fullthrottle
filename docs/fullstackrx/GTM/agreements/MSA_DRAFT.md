**FullStackRx, LLC** [or applicable legal entity], a [STATE] limited liability company ("FullStackRx" or "Company")

## ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

1.1 **"Customer Data"** means all data, including Protected Health Information (PHI), submitted to or processed through the Platform by or on behalf of Customer.

1.2 **"Documentation"** means any user guides, help articles, or technical documentation made available by FullStackRx.

1.3 **"Order Form"** means the order form executed by the Parties that references this Agreement and sets forth the specific subscription terms.

1.4 **"Platform"** means FullStackRx's proprietary compounding pharmacy workflow software-as-a-service platform, including all features, updates, and documentation.

1.5 **"Subscription Fee"** means the platform fee set forth in the Order Form.

1.6 **"Transaction Fee"** means the per-prescription fee set forth in the Order Form, charged per prescription processed through the Platform.

1.7 **"Users"** means Customer's employees, contractors, providers, and patients who are authorized to access the Platform under Customer's account.

## ARTICLE 2: PLATFORM ACCESS AND USE

### 2.1 License Grant
Subject to the terms of this Agreement and timely payment of all fees, FullStackRx grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable, limited right to access and use the Platform during the Subscription Term solely for Customer's internal compounding pharmacy operations.

### 2.2 User Accounts
Customer is responsible for all activity occurring under its accounts. Customer shall: (a) maintain the security of all login credentials; (b) notify FullStackRx promptly of any unauthorized access; and (c) ensure all Users comply with this Agreement.

### 2.3 Restrictions
Customer shall not: (a) sublicense, resell, or transfer the Platform to any third party without prior written consent; (b) reverse engineer, decompile, or attempt to derive source code; (c) use the Platform to build a competing product; (d) remove any proprietary notices; or (e) use the Platform in violation of applicable law.

### 2.4 Acceptable Use
Customer shall use the Platform only for lawful compounding pharmacy operations. Customer is responsible for ensuring all providers and patients using the Platform do so in compliance with applicable laws, including state pharmacy regulations.

## ARTICLE 3: FEES AND PAYMENT

### 3.1 Subscription Fee
Customer shall pay the Subscription Fee set forth in the Order Form. Fees are billed in advance, per location, on a monthly or annual basis as selected in the Order Form.

### 3.2 Transaction Fees
Transaction Fees are charged per prescription processed through the Platform at the rate set forth in the Order Form. Transaction Fees are billed monthly in arrears based on actual usage.

### 3.3 Setup Fee
A one-time setup fee equal to the first month's Subscription Fee is due at signing, unless waived as noted in the Order Form.

### 3.4 Annual Discount
Customers on annual contracts receive a 10% discount on the Subscription Fee. The annual discount may not be combined with any other discount or promotional pricing.

### 3.5 Payment Terms
All fees are due upon invoice. Subscription Fees are collected via the payment method on file through Stripe. FullStackRx reserves the right to suspend access for accounts more than 15 days past due.

### 3.6 Taxes
Fees do not include taxes. Customer is responsible for all applicable taxes, excluding taxes on FullStackRx's net income.

### 3.7 Fee Adjustments
FullStackRx may adjust Subscription Fees upon 60 days' written notice prior to the start of a new Subscription Term. Transaction Fee rates are fixed for the initial contract term and subject to renegotiation at renewal.

### 3.8 Tier Adjustments
Subscription tier is determined by monthly Rx volume as set forth in the Order Form. At each annual renewal, the tier will be reviewed against actual 12-month average volume and adjusted accordingly.

## ARTICLE 4: TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Term
This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for the Initial Term specified in the Order Form, then automatically renews for successive terms (monthly or annual, matching the Initial Term) unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal: (a) 30 days prior to renewal for monthly contracts; or (b) 90 days prior to renewal for annual contracts.

### 4.2 Termination for Cause
Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice if the other Party: (a) materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within 30 days of written notice; or (b) becomes insolvent, makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors, or has a receiver appointed.

### 4.3 Termination for Convenience
Customer may terminate a month-to-month subscription with 30 days' written notice. Annual contracts may not be terminated for convenience prior to the end of the Initial Term; fees for the remaining term are due upon early termination.

### 4.4 Effect of Termination
Upon termination: (a) all licenses granted hereunder terminate immediately; (b) Customer shall cease all use of the Platform; (c) FullStackRx will make Customer Data available for export for 30 days, after which it will be deleted in accordance with the BAA; and (d) all accrued payment obligations survive termination.

## ARTICLE 5: DATA AND SECURITY

### 5.1 Customer Data Ownership
Customer retains all right, title, and interest in and to Customer Data. FullStackRx's right to use Customer Data is limited to providing the Platform and services described herein.

### 5.2 Security
FullStackRx shall implement and maintain commercially reasonable administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect Customer Data, consistent with the requirements of the HIPAA Security Rule and the BAA.

### 5.3 HIPAA
The Parties acknowledge that Customer Data may include Protected Health Information subject to HIPAA. The Business Associate Agreement ("BAA"), attached as Exhibit B to the Order Form, is incorporated herein and governs the handling of PHI.

### 5.4 Data Processing
FullStackRx may use aggregated, de-identified data derived from Customer Data to improve the Platform, provided such data cannot be used to identify Customer or any individual.

### 6.2 Obligations

## ARTICLE 12: GENERAL PROVISIONS

### 12.1 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Utah, without regard to its conflict of law principles.

### 12.2 Dispute Resolution
The Parties shall attempt to resolve disputes in good faith. If unresolved within 30 days, disputes shall be submitted to binding arbitration in Salt Lake City, Utah under the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

### 12.3 Entire Agreement
This Agreement, together with the Order Form and BAA, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior agreements relating to the subject matter hereof.

### 12.4 Amendment
FullStackRx may update these terms upon 30 days' written notice. Customer's continued use of the Platform after notice constitutes acceptance.

### 12.5 Waiver
Failure by either Party to enforce any provision shall not constitute a waiver of future enforcement.

### 12.6 Severability
If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions continue in full force.

### 12.7 Assignment
Customer may not assign this Agreement without FullStackRx's prior written consent. FullStackRx may assign this Agreement in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

### 12.8 Force Majeure
Neither Party shall be liable for delays or failures caused by circumstances beyond its reasonable control, including natural disasters, acts of government, or internet outages.

### 12.9 Notices
Notices shall be in writing and delivered via email (with confirmation of receipt) or certified mail to the addresses set forth in the Order Form.

### 12.10 No Third-Party Beneficiaries
This Agreement does not confer any rights on any third party.
